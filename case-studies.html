Performance Optimization for a Subscription-Based Business

Project Overview

A growing subscription-based business lacked structured KPI tracking and clear visibility into churn, recurring revenue trends, and customer lifetime value. Reporting was fragmented, limiting strategic decision-making.

Performance Gap

Before

  • Fragmented KPI tracking
  • No churn visibility
  • Revenue growth misinterpreted
  • Inconsistent reporting cadence

After

  • Aligned KPI framework
  • Structured churn modeling
  • Clear revenue segmentation
  • Executive-ready reporting system

Objective

  • Establish aligned performance metrics
  • Improve recurring revenue visibility
  • Identify churn drivers and retention gaps
  • Create structured executive dashboard

Approach

  1. Cleaned and structured transactional data
  2. Defined core KPIs (MRR, churn rate, CLV, growth rate)
  3. Modeled retention and revenue trends
  4. Built structured performance dashboard

High-Value Cohort Retention

Insights Identified

  • Churn exceeded sustainable threshold in Q2
  • Revenue growth driven primarily by acquisition rather than retention
  • Customer lifetime value miscalculated in prior reporting
  • High-value cohorts displayed stronger retention patterns

Outcome

The engagement established structured KPI alignment, improved executive reporting clarity, and provided actionable insights for revenue stabilization and long-term growth strategy.
